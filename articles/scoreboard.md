## Current Rankings

**gmrr**, **vol**, and **sharpe** are calculated on a per-trading day
basis using end-of-day Net Asset Values of each trader’s account. See
[the scoring
section](https://fintechtradingcompetition.com/articles/Scoring.html)
for an in-depth explaination.

- **rank**: overall ranking in the competition. Your tier appears as the
  whole number and your ranking within your tier appears as a decimal;
  for example, Trader 3.4 is ranked \#4 in the 3rd tier.

- **gmrr**: Geometric mean of rate of return

- **vol**: Volatility of returns

- **sharpe**: (*gmrr* - *rf*) / *vol*
