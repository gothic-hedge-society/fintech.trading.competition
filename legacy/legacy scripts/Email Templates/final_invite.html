Duke Fintech Trading Competition 2023!

The Duke Master of Engineering in Financial Technology (FinTech) program, in collaboration with the Student Trading Lab at Interactive Brokers, is excited to announce the 2023 FINTECH Trading Competition to take place during the 2023 Fall Semester! Read the official press release here and note that the deadline has been extended to 06 Oct.

Overview

The Trading Competition gives university students the opportunity to develop and try out investment strategies by trading real financial assets - using real-time market data - without committing actual capital. Participating students will be issued $1 million in simulated currency within a paper trading account at Interactive Brokers (NYSE: IBKR, https://www.interactivebrokers.com/). Students will make trades within their paper accounts and gain exposure to the same user interface and features utilized by live accounts at Interactive Brokers.
